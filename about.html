Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Global Scale

At Vnik, we are storytellers of India's rich culinary heritage. Founded with a vision to bring the finest Indian products to the world, we supply authentic, high-quality teas, spices, snacks, grains, and health foods to global buyers. Every product reflects the values behind the brand: quality, royalty, and health.

Global Reach

Regular exports to Europe, USA, Middle East, Australia, Mauritius and beyond. Trusted by importers, distributors, and retailers worldwide.

Quality Assured

Each batch tested, verified and packed under hygienic conditions. APEDA registered, FSSAI approved with full documentation support.

Supply Chain

End-to-end logistics from packaging to cold chain, freight coordination, customs clearance and real-time shipment tracking.

Private Label Ready

OEM, white-label, bulk and multilingual retail packaging support. Your brand, our supply with full customization options.

Product Range

From Darjeeling and Assam single origin teas to Bihar foxnut 8++ suta, cardamom, cloves, Sri Lankan cinnamon, black pepper and dehydrated foods.

Quick Lead Times
