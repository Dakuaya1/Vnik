Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Global Scale

At Vnik, we are storytellers of India's rich culinary heritage. Founded with a vision to bring the finest Indian products to the world, we supply authentic, high-quality teas, spices, snacks, grains, and health foods to global buyers.

Every product reflects the values behind the brand: quality, royalty, and health. From Darjeeling and Assam single origin teas to Bihar foxnut 8++ suta, premium cardamom, cloves, Sri Lankan cinnamon, black pepper, and dehydrated fruits and vegetables, Vnik is built for dependable international supply.
